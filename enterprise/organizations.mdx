## Overview

Organizations are the top of the Astralform tenant hierarchy: **Organization → Team → Project**. An organization owns one or more teams, and enterprise controls — SAML SSO and SCIM provisioning — bind at the organization level.

Every developer gets a **personal organization** automatically on sign-up. Existing teams were migrated under their owner's organization, so nothing changes about how you access projects — the organization simply sits above the teams you already have.

## Organization Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control — manage members, transfer ownership, delete the organization, and manage SSO and SCIM |
| **Admin** | Manage members and SSO/SCIM configuration (cannot delete the organization) |
| **Member** | Access the organization and its teams (no admin surface) |

## Managing an Organization

Open the **Organizations** section in the dashboard sidebar to manage your organization.

### Members

Owners and admins can:

- **Invite** a member by email (the user must already have an Astralform account)
- Change a member's **role** (owner, admin, or member)
- **Remove** a member from the organization

### Settings

- **Name** — the organization's display name
- SSO and SCIM configuration (owner/admin only)

### Switching Organizations

Use the **organization switcher** at the top of the Organizations section to move between organizations you belong to — for example, your personal organization and a company organization you were invited to.

## Relationship to Teams

Teams belong to an organization, and a team's members are also members of that team's organization. The hierarchy flows downward:

- **Organizations** own teams and hold enterprise settings (SSO, SCIM)
- **[Teams](/dashboard/teams)** group projects and control membership
- **[Projects](/dashboard/projects)** hold agents, API keys, and tool configuration
