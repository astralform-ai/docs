## Overview

Teams are the top-level organizational unit in Astralform. Every project belongs to a team, and team membership controls who can access and manage projects.

When you create an account, a default personal team is created automatically.

## Team Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control — manage members, transfer ownership, delete team |
| **Admin** | Create projects, manage members (cannot add admins or delete team) |
| **Member** | Access team projects, view members |

## Creating a Team

1. Go to [astralform.ai](https://astralform.ai)
2. Click the team switcher in the sidebar
3. Click **Create Team**
4. Enter a team name and slug (URL-friendly identifier)

The slug must be lowercase alphanumeric with hyphens, 2-60 characters (e.g., `my-startup`).

## Managing Members

### Adding Members

Team admins and owners can add members by email. The invited user must already have an Astralform account.

### Roles and Permissions

- **Owners** can promote members to admin, remove admins, and transfer ownership
- **Admins** can add/remove members (not other admins)
- **Members** can leave the team voluntarily

### Transferring Ownership

Only the current owner can transfer ownership. The previous owner is demoted to admin.

## Team Icon

Upload a custom team icon (PNG, JPEG, or WebP, max 256 KB) from team settings.
